About Ken

Born in the Philippines, Ken has been an artist his entire life. After moving to California in 2002, he worked his way through different mediums before finding his passion for the craft of tattooing December of 2020. With a hunger to learn more, and some experience under his belt, Ken specializes in black & grey work, mainly in realism pieces.
